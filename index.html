Escape Room Puzzle: Defeat T-Robot

T-Robot, a malicious mechanical guardian, has locked you in a state-of-the-art room.
To escape, you must solve a series of technology-related challenges.
Each challenge you master provides a crucial key to unlock the escape room door and secure your freedom.

After completing each challenge, you will receive an envelope with a number.
The codes in the envelopes form the password needed to disable T-Robot’s defense mechanisms.

⚠️ You only have three (3) attempts!
